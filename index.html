Our Menu

Chicken

Explore our variety of chicken dishes, each one infused with rich flavors and cooked to juicy perfection. Try our classic herb-roasted chicken, smothered in a savory gravy, or our spicy buffalo wings that pack a punch.

Beef

Our beef menu offers a selection of succulent steaks and hearty stews. Indulge in a tender filet mignon, char-grilled to your liking, or savor our slow-cooked beef bourguignon, rich with red wine and aromatics.

Sushi

Delight in our sushi offerings, featuring fresh, expertly sliced fish atop seasoned rice. From the classic California roll to the adventurous dragon roll, each piece is a work of art.
